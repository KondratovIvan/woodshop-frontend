Delivery Information

1. Post Office Delivery

Choose delivery to your local post office branch. Once your package arrives, you’ll receive a notification with the tracking number and pickup instructions. This method is perfect if you prefer to collect your order at your convenience.


2. Courier Delivery

Prefer door-to-door service? Select courier delivery, and your order will be delivered directly to your address by a trusted postal courier. You’ll be notified in advance with the estimated delivery time and tracking details.


3. In-Store Pickup

You can also pick up your order directly from our store. Simply select the “In-Store Pickup” option at checkout and come by our shop at your convenience once your order is ready. We’ll notify you as soon as it’s available for pickup.

We work with reliable postal services to ensure fast and secure shipping. If you have any questions, feel free to contact our support team.
