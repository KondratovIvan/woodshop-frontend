Payment Information

1. Online Payment via MonoPay

To pay online, select the “Pay Online” option at checkout and click “Proceed to Payment.” You will be securely redirected to MonoPay’s payment gateway, where you can enter your card or e‑wallet details. Once the transaction is approved, you’ll receive an email confirmation with your order number and payment receipt.

• Instant confirmation: as soon as your payment is processed, our system automatically reserves your items and prepares them for shipment.
• Secure processing: all payments go through 3D Secure and SSL encryption to keep your financial data safe.
• Receipts & invoices: you can download your electronic invoice directly from the confirmation email or from your account dashboard.

2. Cash on Delivery (Pay by Cash)

Prefer to pay when you receive your order? Choose “Cash on Delivery” at checkout. Our courier will bring your package right to your door, and you can pay the exact amount in cash upon receipt.

• No pre‑payment required: you only pay when your order arrives.
• Order confirmation: even with cash payment, you’ll get an SMS and email confirming your purchase details and estimated delivery time.
• Change & refunds: please have exact change ready; if you need to return or exchange, our courier will handle cash refunds or collect the new payment on the spot.
