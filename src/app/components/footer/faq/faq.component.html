Frequently Asked Questions

1. How do I create an account?

Click “Login” in the top‑right corner of any page, enter your name, surname, email and a secure password, then confirm via the link we send to your inbox. Once verified, you can manage your profile, view past orders and save favorite products for fast checkout.

You can also sign in using Google for one‑click access. If you ever forget your password, use the “Forgot Password” link to reset it instantly.

2. How can I track my order?

After your order ships, you’ll receive an email and SMS with a tracking number and carrier details. Visit the “My Orders” section in your account dashboard, click the relevant order and follow the “Track Package” link to see real‑time updates on delivery status and estimated arrival time.

3. What is your warranty policy?

All WoodShop products come with a 12‑month warranty against manufacturing defects. If your item malfunctions under normal use, contact our Warranty Team at warranty@woodshop.com within the warranty period to arrange repair or replacement. Proof of purchase will be required.

4. How can I subscribe to your newsletter?

Scroll to the bottom of any page and enter your email in the “Subscribe” box, then click the arrow icon. You’ll receive a welcome email with 10% off your first order and periodic updates on new arrivals, exclusive deals and styling tips. You can unsubscribe at any time via the link in our emails.
