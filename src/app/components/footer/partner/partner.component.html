Become a Partner

1. Reach Out to Us

Interested suppliers should send an email to partners@woodshop.com with a brief company introduction, product catalog, and your website or portfolio link.

2. Meet Our Criteria

We look for partners who offer high‑quality, sustainably sourced wood products, maintain consistent stock levels, and comply with all safety and regulatory standards in Ukraine and the EU.

3. Submit Required Documentation

After initial contact, you’ll be asked to provide your company registration documents, quality certificates, and sample product images for review.

4. Partnership Agreement & Onboarding

Once approved, we’ll send a partnership agreement outlining terms, pricing, and delivery schedules. After signing, our team will onboard you onto our supplier portal and integrate your catalog into WoodShop’s system.
