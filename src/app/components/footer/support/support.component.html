Technical Support

1. Contacting Technical Support

To reach our Technical Support team, email us at support@woodshop.com. We are available Monday–Friday from 9 am to 9 pm, on Saturday from 9 am to 7 pm and on Sunday from 00:30 am to 6 pm. and aim to respond within 24 hours.

2. Order & Delivery Assistance

We can help track your shipment, resolve delivery delays or address changes, and provide updated tracking information for your order.

3. Payment & Billing Support

Our team assists with payment failures, billing discrepancies, invoice requests, and can guide you through the checkout process.

4. Account & Profile Issues

We offer support for login problems, password resets, account verification, and updating personal or shipping information on your profile.

5. Website & Technical Issues

Facing errors on the site? We troubleshoot website bugs, broken links, image‑loading issues, and optimize performance for a smooth shopping experience.
