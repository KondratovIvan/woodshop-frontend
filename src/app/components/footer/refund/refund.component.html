Purchase Refund

1. Contact Customer Support

To start your refund, please contact our Technical Support team at support@woodshop.com or via live chat on our website. We’re available Monday–Friday from 9 am to 9 pm, on Saturday from 9 am to 7 pm and on Sunday from 00:30 am to 6 pm.

2. Provide Proof of Purchase

Send us your electronic receipt or upload a clear photo of the physical invoice you received with your order. This helps us verify your purchase quickly.

3. Return the Item

Pack the item securely in its original packaging and drop it off at any post office. Please use a trackable shipping method and keep the tracking number for your records.

4. Submit Bank Details

Email us the bank card number or IBAN where you’d like the refund to be issued. Ensure the account name matches the name on your order to avoid delays.

5. Refund Processing

Once we receive and inspect the returned item, we will process your refund within 5–7 business days. You’ll receive a confirmation email with the transaction details.
